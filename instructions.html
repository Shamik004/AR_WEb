How to Use the AR Drawing App

1. Start the Webcam Feed: Click the "Start Webcam Feed" button to begin the video feed from your camera.

2. Draw in the Air: Use hand gestures to draw in the air. The app will recognize your gestures and create a drawing based on them.

3. Pause and Save: Use the control buttons to pause drawing and save your artwork. Saved drawings will be uploaded to Firebase.

4. Control Buttons: Use the "Clear" button to start a new drawing and the "Play" button to resume drawing.
